📝 Frontmatter for Edgework Posts#

Each post starts with structured frontmatter that helps organize and display content correctly:

---
title: Algorithmic Ghosts
published: 2025-03-15
description: A reflection on invisible labor in Responsible AI.
image: ./cover.jpg
tags: [Responsible AI, Ethics, Autoethnography]
category: Edgework Essays
draft: false
---
AttributeDescription
titleThe post title as it will appear on the site.
publishedThe publication date (format: YYYY-MM-DD).
descriptionShort summary of the post. Displayed on homepage and lists.
imageCover image path:
• http(s):// = remote image
• / = from public/ folder
• Otherwise = relative to the markdown file
tagsKeywords that describe the content. Used for filtering and discovery.
categoryGroups the post under a major theme (e.g. Edgework Essays, Reflections, Interviews).
draftIf true, the post will not appear on the public site. Useful for drafts or future work.

Where to Place the Post Files#

Your post files should be placed in src/content/posts/ directory. You can also create sub-directories to better organize your posts and assets.

src/content/posts/
├── algorithmic-ghosts.md
└── ai-interview/
    ├── image.png
    └── index.md
